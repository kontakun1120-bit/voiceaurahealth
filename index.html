Vision

VoiceAuraHealth explores how human voice carries information about health, emotional states, and stress levels. Our goal is to build an AI platform that helps detect mental and physical conditions through voice analysis.

Technology

The platform combines AI, signal processing, and voice feature extraction to understand patterns hidden inside speech signals. VoiceAuraHealth is an experimental research project exploring the future of voice-based health analysis.
